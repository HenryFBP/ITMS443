Kristofer Luck

Kristofer Luck was born in 1978. Kristofer currently lives in Chicago. Kristofer Luck attended St. Patrick High School in Chicago, IL and graduated class of 1996. After high school, he went to Wilbur Wright College in Chicago, IL. he attended college from 1996 to 2000.

Work History: Quest Network Services
